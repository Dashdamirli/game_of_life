Finds the alive neighbor count.

Checks all cells around the position and based on parameter of users choice find neighbors for circular or clipped integration. for circular it checks the corresponding neighbors, mainly its parallels for sides and its reverse corner for diagonal. For example, if the neighbor is out of boundaries from the top right corner it is gonna shift it and check the bottom left corner

Parameters
arraysRowthe x posotion of the arena.
arraysColumnthe y posotion of the arena.
2D_Arraythe arena.
isCircularBoolean to for circulator or clipped arenas.
Returns
number of alive neighbors.
Warning
the 4th parameter takes 0 or 1 c.
